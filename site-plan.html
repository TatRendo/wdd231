Site Plan: Pet Love

Site Name

Pet Love was chosen because it reflects the mission of the site: connecting pets with loving homes. It's memorable, emotionally engaging, and communicates empathy.

Site Purpose

Pet Love is a platform dedicated to promoting responsible pet adoption. The site will showcase featured animals, upcoming adoption events, an online application form, live weather updates for outdoor events, and links to social media and shelter locations.

Scenarios

  • Where can I see pets that need urgent adoption?
  • How can I register for the next adoption event?

Color Scheme

  • #d35400 (orange): used for headings, buttons, and accents
  • #fdf6f0 (light beige): used for the background

These are the colors but you can change them according to how you see them..

Typography

  • Segoe UI: used for body text for its readability and modern style
  • Roboto Slab: used for headings for its elegance and contrast

This document uses Segoe UI as the primary font.
